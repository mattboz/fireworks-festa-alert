What time do the fireworks usually start?

Exact times are rarely published in advance and can vary village to village, but the typical mortali (aerial fireworks) pattern is:

  • Morning round — usually around 8am, most days of the festa.
  • Evening round — on the main festa days.
  • Friday, Saturday and Sunday of festa weekend typically get both a morning and an evening round.

Treat this as a general expectation, not a guarantee — band clubs set their own schedules and these aren't always officially published. If your dog is very noise-sensitive, plan for the whole festa week, not just the titular day.
